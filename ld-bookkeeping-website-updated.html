About LD Bookkeeping

We’re based in Milton Keynes and support clients across the UK. Whether you're a self-employed tradesperson, a delivery driver, or a small business owner, our goal is to keep your finances clear and compliant — so you can focus on what you do best.

We are certified QuickBooks ProAdvisors, meaning you’re in expert hands when it comes to setup, training, and ongoing support on QuickBooks Online.

Our founder has several years of experience working in finance across a range of sectors including manufacturing, FMCG, and international development. He has worked in both SMEs and large global businesses with over 10,000 employees. His background includes financial analysis, reporting, budgeting, inventory accounting, and working with SAP and Power BI — all of which feed into the practical and structured approach we take to helping our clients today.
